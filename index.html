Scientific fundamentals & warnings

Solid reagents

Mass is computed from the target molarity (M), final volume (L), and molecular weight (g/mol).

m (g) = M · V · MW

Outputs are auto-formatted with practical prefixes: g → mg → μg, L → mL → μL.

Liquid reagents

For concentrated liquids, stock molarity is estimated using density and purity, then used to compute the required stock volume.

M1 = (P · ρ · 1000) / MW
V1 = (M2 · V2) / M1

Practical warnings

This tool is a calculation aid. Always verify units, reagent labels, and SOP constraints before preparing solutions.
